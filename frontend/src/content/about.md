House Lights is an events tracker for Amsterdam stages: theatre, comedy, dance, music, opera, and whatever else is on. It pulls in listings from 25+ venues and companies, so there's one place to see what's actually happening this week without juggling a dozen different websites.

The site is curated in the sense that I've hand-picked which venues to include, ranked them by how likely I am to go, and the watchlist is totally mine. Think of it less like a city-wide listings site and more like a friend who follows the Amsterdam performance arts scene too closely.

This is a side project I'm actively building, not a finished product. The scrapers run on my machine, so if listings go stale for a few days it's probably because my laptop was closed. There's also a Discover tab that lets you (me) search by mood or vibe using AI — I'm still experimenting so it's not available online. 

If you'd like me to turn this into a proper app (with live data, personal accounts, the AI stuff actually in the cloud, and more) let me know. 

And if you know of a venue or company I'm missing, or have any other suggestions, I'd love to hear about that too. 

You probably know how to find me.

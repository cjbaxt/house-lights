House Lights is an events tracker for Amsterdam stages: theatre, comedy, dance, music, opera, and whatever else is on. It pulls in listings from 25+ venues and companies, so there's one place to see what's actually happening without juggling a dozen different websites.

- **Browse** — everything coming up, filtered by type, timeframe, or specific venue.
- **Watchlist** — bookmark shows you're interested in or have bought tickets for, and export them to your calendar. Stored in your browser, so don't clear your cache.
- **Discover** — AI mood search: describe what you're in the mood for and it'll suggest something. Only available locally for now (it runs against a model on my machine).

This is a side project I'm actively building. The scrapers run on my machine, so if listings go stale for a few days, my laptop was probably closed. And it's curated by me in the sense that I've hand-picked which venues to include.
